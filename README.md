## ✅ Before You Post

- 🔍 **Search first** — your question may already be answered
- 🎯 **Use the right category** — it helps staff find and respond to you faster
- 🤝 **Be respectful** — toxic behavior gets threads (and players) locked
- 📝 **Be clear & specific** — especially for appeals and applications
